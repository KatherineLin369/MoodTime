- Chat integration endpoints: /api/conversations, /api/conversations/:id/messages
- Mood endpoints assumed: GET/POST /api/moods
- Images endpoints assumed: /api/generate-image
- Design system: Soft pastels, rounded corners, "safe space" aesthetic
